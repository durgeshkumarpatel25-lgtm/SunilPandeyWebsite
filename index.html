Why Choose LIC?

The Life Insurance Corporation of India is more than just insurance; it's a promise of trust, backed by the sovereign guarantee of the Government of India.

Sovereign Guarantee

Unlike private insurers, LIC policies carry a sovereign guarantee under Section 37 of the LIC Act, ensuring absolute safety of your sum assured and bonuses.

Unmatched Claim Ratio

LIC consistently maintains one of the highest claim settlement ratios globally (over 98%), ensuring your family faces no hurdles during times of need.

Consistent Bonuses

Decades of proven track record in declaring attractive bonuses on participating policies, beating inflation and building generational wealth.
